Project Introduction and Background

The Campus Companion -- USIU Smart Campus Helper is a web application designed to enhance the university experience by integrating information and services for students. It addresses the issue of unstructured access to fundamental campus-related information such as class schedules, events, navigation, and notices.

Currently, students utilize disconnected channels including emails, notice boards, and WhatsApp groups, which causes missed information and inefficiency. The Campus Companion aims to consolidate these into one accessible platform, promoting efficiency and improved communication within the university community.

Problem Statement

Universities tend to have the problem of effective communication and information dissemination between students and staff. At USIU-Africa, important announcements such as class changes, event alerts, and general important notices are relayed via multiple, often uncoordinated platforms.

This promotes fragmentation, confusion, missed announcements, and inefficiencies in daily campus life. Students also find it difficult to locate buildings or classrooms, especially new students who are unfamiliar with the campus terrain. A centralized digital solution that simplifies access to campus information, consolidates navigation assistance, and enhances the overall student experience is urgently needed.

Project Objectives

  • To create a smart campus web application that centralizes communication, navigation, and scheduling for USIU students and staff
  • To bring campus information systems into one web-based system
  • To enhance the efficiency of campus communications and event management
  • To establish an interactive campus map and chatbot assistant to find places in the campus and respond to inquiries
  • To enable efficient dissemination of announcements, class schedules, and alerts
